SCAMSCAN

Overview

Scam Scan is an AI-powered scam filtering app that helps you stay protected from scams by scanning SMS, emails, and call histories for suspicious keywords or patterns, such as fake OTPs or URLs. With Machine Learning, Scam Scan adapts to new scam tactics, helping you stay one step ahead of scammers.

Education and Awareness Hub

Engage in interactive training modules with real-life scam scenarios and quizzes. Earn points as you practice identifying scams, and exchange points for vouchers, in collaboration with NCPC.

Alerts

Stay informed with Monthly Scam Analysis Reports on scam trends, especially for young working adults and high-risk job sectors. Receive personalized alerts based on your demographic or financial habits.

Community Scams

Submit details about your scam encounters to help others. Suspicious links are directly sent to authorities for quick action. The Peer Alert system also notifies you if someone in your network is targeted.
